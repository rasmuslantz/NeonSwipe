Privacy Policy

Neon Swipe is designed to respect your privacy. Here’s how we handle your data.

On-device processing

Your photos and videos are processed locally on your device. We do not upload or store your library on our servers.

Ads & connectivity

The app requires an internet connection to load ads. No photo content is sent with ads. If you purchase Premium, ads are removed.

Purchases

If you buy a subscription, Apple/Google handles the transaction and may store necessary purchase information per their policies.
