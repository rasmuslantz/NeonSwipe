Can’t access Photos?

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Neon Swipe → Allow “All Photos”.

Undo a deletion?

Open Photos → Albums → Recently Deleted (available ~30 days).

Storage not changing?

After a cleanup, empty “Recently Deleted” to reclaim storage immediately.

Restore purchases

Open Neon Swipe → Settings → Restore Purchases.

Neon Swipe processes your photos on-device. See our privacy policy on the App Store.
